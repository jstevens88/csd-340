Cooking is definitely one of my favorite ways to relax after a long day. Most people probably don't come home from a long day of work looking forward to preparing a fresh meal. Something about looking around the kitchen and thinking about what I could make with what we have really gets me excited about the endless possibilities!

I don't think I can remember the last time I visited him where there WASN'T an insanely difficuly puzzle on his custom made puzzle table!
